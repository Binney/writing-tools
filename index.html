    Nouns and Verbs

    Why?

    Stephen King says adjectives and adverbs are rubbish. If you have something to say, all you should need is a noun and a verb. If you choose the right noun and the right verb, everything else just falls into place.

    Use this generator to produce a noun/verb combination and write a story from there. Keep it concise!
